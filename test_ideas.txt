State 22: Pandora.
Pandora is a dormant state, represented by Deep Pink `#ff1493`.
Pandora cells are placed on the board with a small chance (0.01%) when creating a new board, and they rarely spawn from the void (0.01% chance).
Pandora sits completely still.
If Pandora is touched by any Kaiju (Godzilla, Jaeger, Mothra, MechaGodzilla, Omega, Reaper, Phoenix, Nidhogg), it opens.
When Pandora opens, it catastrophically alters the board. The cell it is on becomes a Wormhole, and in a 5x5 area around it, EVERY cell is turned into a randomized state with equal probabilities for all 22 states (including Kaiju, Yggdrasils, Glitches, Supernovas, etc.). This simulates unleashing absolute chaos upon the universe.
